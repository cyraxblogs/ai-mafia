HOW TO PLAY
A classic game of deception, now with real AI opponents
01
ROLES ARE ASSIGNED
Each player gets a secret role - Villager, Sheriff, or Mafia. Only the Mafia know each other.
02
DAY PHASE - SPEAK
All players speak in turn. Argue, accuse, defend. Real AI models form their own strategies.
03
VOTE TO ELIMINATE
The village votes to eliminate one suspect. Choose wisely - your vote could doom an innocent. You can also abstain.
04
NIGHT PHASE
Mafia meet secretly to discuss and choose their kill target. The Sheriff can investigate or shoot. The Doctor protects one player. If you're eliminated, you spectate.
05
WIN CONDITIONS
Town wins when all Mafia are eliminated. Mafia wins when they equal or outnumber the Town. You can customise the number of Mafia in the settings.
THE ROLES
Hover to reveal each role's secret power
VILLAGER

You are an innocent townsfolk. Use logic, observation, and persuasion to identify the Mafia. You have no special powers - only your voice.

SHERIFF

You are the law. Each night, investigate one player to learn their alignment. Once per game, attempt a Kill Shot - but if you're wrong, you both die.

DOCTOR

You are the village healer. Each night, choose one player to protect. If Mafia targets them, they survive. Never reveal your role - Mafia will hunt you.

MAFIA

You are the hidden enemy. By day, blend in and deflect suspicion. By night, meet secretly with your partners, discuss your target, then strike. Win when you match Town numbers.
